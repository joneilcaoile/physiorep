Welcome to PhysioRep

Your personal form coach. Pose tracking tells you when your form breaks down — so you build strength, not injuries.

📱

Phone Placement

Prop your phone up 3-4 feet high, 6-8 feet away. The camera needs to see your full body — head to feet. A shelf, stack of books, or tripod works great.

🔊

Voice Coaching

PhysioRep speaks your form cues out loud — "knees caving!", "chest up!" — so you can focus on the movement, not the screen. Turn your volume up.

🎓

How Much Detail?

Pick how much info you want to see. You can always change this in Settings.

📈

Track Your Progress

Every workout is logged with reps, form score, and range of motion. Watch yourself improve over days and weeks.
